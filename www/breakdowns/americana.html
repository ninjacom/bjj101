AMERICANA FROM MOUNT

Instruction: To do the Americana from the Mount, it is very important to twist your body in a position where you have balance. Now, you're gonna force his arm; I use my shoulder to push on his arm so he'll bring his hand to me. Then I grab his wrist, and grab my own wrist. Once I finish it up by pushing his shoulder, I'm gonna pull his arm backwards and finish the Americana.
